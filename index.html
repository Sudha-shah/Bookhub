પુરુષ પ્રધાન સમાજ મા નારીનું સ્થાન


પુરુષ પ્રધાન સમાજ મા નારીનું સ્થાન is a beautiful book written by Sudha Shah. She discusses the position of women in a male-dominated society, highlighting issues such as early marriage and motherhood, and the need for female empowerment. The piece reflects on cultural attitudes towards women, urging a shift towards respect and equality, and celebrating women’s achievements and potential.
